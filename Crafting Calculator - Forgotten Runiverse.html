Welcome to the Crafting Calculator

Embark on your crafting journey in Forgotten Runiverse with this elegant tool, designed to transform your inventory into powerful gear, spells, and decorations.

How to Use

  • Enter Your Inventory: Visit the Resources tab (Gems, Metals, etc.) and Items tab (Shards, Essences, etc.). Input the quantities of all materials you possess to ensure precise crafting calculations.
  • Select Categories: Choose Gear, Spell, and/or Decoration to filter craftable items. Select all for a complete list.
  • Choose Match Mode: Use Full Match for items you can craft now, or Partial Match to see items missing one material or with up to two materials short of the required quantity.
  • Discover Craftable Items: Click “Calculate Craftable Items” to reveal what you can craft. The calculator divides your material quantities by the required amounts to determine how many items you can create.
  • Craft Specific Items: In the Crafting tab, select an item and quantity to view its material and gold requirements.
  • Check Remaining Materials: Select items to craft, then click “Calculate Remainder” to see what materials remain in your inventory.

Why Use It?

Maximize your crafting efficiency by ensuring every resource and item is accounted for. Missing materials (e.g., Shards) can lead to incomplete results, so input your full inventory to unlock all crafting possibilities.

Tips for Mastery

  • Always include both resources and items for accurate results.
  • Use Partial Match to plan your next gathering trip for one missing material or to top up short quantities.
  • Experiment with different items in the Crafting tab to plan your next craft.
  • Use the material modal to find where to gather more resources or defeat enemies for drops.
